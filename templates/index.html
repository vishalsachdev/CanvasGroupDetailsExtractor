Canvas Course Group Details Extractor

The Canvas Course Group Details Extractor is a powerful tool designed to help educators and administrators easily access and analyze course data from Canvas. It extracts student information including names, emails, and group assignments, as well as group details such as names and member counts. The tool presents data in an organized tabular format and allows for easy export to CSV. We prioritize data privacy by only collecting necessary information, processing it in-memory, and immediately deleting any temporary data after export. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with their institution's data privacy policies and relevant regulations.
